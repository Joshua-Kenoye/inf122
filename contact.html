Frequently Asked Questions

When are office hours?

Office hours are held Monday through Friday from 10 AM to 4 PM. You can also schedule appointments outside these times.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters may receive faster responses.

Can I ask questions about assignments?

Absolutely! We encourage students to ask questions about course material, assignments, and projects. Use the contact form or visit during office hours.

Is there a discussion forum?

Yes! Check the course portal for the discussion forum where you can interact with classmates and instructors.

Contact us as soon as possible to reschedule. We'll do our best to find a mutually convenient time.
